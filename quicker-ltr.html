Quicker continues in the revamped version

מהיר יותר | חוסך זמן | מותאם אישית

The National Authority for Public Transportation in the Ministry of Transportation decided to continue operating a service Quicker  As a complementary and additional service to the regular public transportation bus lines in the Hefer Valley and northern Lev Hasharon area. In the new model, the operating area will be divided into 3 sub-areas ("complexes") and it will be possible to drive in only one area, without crossing into another complex.

One of Quicker's main goals is to make accessible areas where regular public transportation coverage is low or non-existent and thus connect them efficiently and quickly to the city centers in the region and to the centers of demand in the region.

The new model will allow shorter trips, which will allow greater availability of vehicles Quicker  within the designated areas. All the new complexes border main thoroughfares with a high frequency of bus lines so that it is possible to travel with Quicker  and change to a bus that will take you to a destination outside the complex.

As an additional service to the regular public transportation service, which offers a service that is tailored exactly to you, unlike a regular bus line, and similar to other areas where a service like this operates, prices Quicker  will be charged and payment for the service will be via credit card, which must be entered in the application in advance.

We will soon be renewing ourselves with a modern vehicle fleet which, in a unprecedented manner, will also be fully accessible, providing service to people who move around using wheelchairs.

Interested in getting to your destination by shared transportation?

  • Download the appMOOVITand choose an origin and destination.
  • Choose a serviceQuickerof the lines company. New and existing users will be asked to enter the details of the credit card that will be charged for the trip and the mobile phone number they use.
  • Choose the desired departure time and the system will offer you a ride, a pick-up station with an estimated pick-up and arrival time.
  • Confirm the travel details and receive an estimated pickup time.
  • Towards the time of the trip, we will send you a message with the exact details of the trip. through an appMOOVITYou will be able to be updated in real time on the location of the ride that is on the way to you so that you can wait for the ride on time at the pickup station.
  • DriverQuickerwill wait for the passenger at the station at the scheduled pick-up time. To avoid delays for the other passengers, we will wait one minute after the appointed time. As long as the passenger(s) do not arrive at the station on time, we will have to continue to the next station, so as not to be late for the passengers waiting further down the road.

Attention!

  • Due to the change of the model to a model by complexes it is possible that trips you have made in the past will no longer be possible in the new booking system ofQuicker. We ask you to keep up to date with the new polygon map below in order to familiarize yourself with the various options for placing an order.
  • You can book a trip immediately atQuickerOr later that day and even the next day.
  • Are we late for the ride? It is important to us to be on time and pick you up on time, but there may be delays due to various reasons that are sometimes beyond our control. We're more than 10 minutes late, we'll drive you to your destination free of charge. We were more than 20 minutes late, we will not charge you for the trip and we will give you credit for the next trip with us.
  • Senior citizens are entitled to a 50% discount on the cost of travel.All that is required is to enter a senior citizen placement profile in the MOOVIT app under "My Account" along with an ID. After the profile is approved, the credit card will be charged half of the fare automatically.

When the "Rav-Ko" card is lost/stolen/broken the card holder will be responsible for issuing a new Rab-Ko card at a cost of 10 NIS. If balances remain on the old card, they will be transferred to the new card up to 72 hours after the last use of the card.

Is it necessary to validate the "Rav-Ko" card even if I purchased a free monthly/weekly/daily subscription?

"Accumulated value" is a travel contract by loading the amount of money on the multi-line card. Amounts can be charged in multiples of 30, 50, 100, 150 and 200 NIS for use on all public group lines and there is no time limit. For each trip, the ticket is charged at the price of a single trip, which is deducted from the accumulated amount. If the passenger is entitled to a discount, it is given at the time of loading the accumulated value, according to his personal eligibility profile updated on the multi-line card. *There is no refund for accumulated value.

You can check with the school where the child is studying if it is possible to issue the "Rav-Ko" card through the secretariat. If not, the child must be accompanied by a parent including the ID card of the card holder. Issuing a ticket to a child under 16 years of age will be done by the parents in the presence of the child, the parents will present a ID card with an original attachment. The card holder must be present at the issuance, it is not possible to scan a photo. And you are entitled to the discount, you must have a certificate proving eligibility.
